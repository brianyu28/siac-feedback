Who We Are

We (Pleasanton SIAC) are an action-oriented student council in the Pleasanton Unified School District (PUSD).

Purpose

The Pleasanton Student Inter-schools Action Council supports the power of students to transform their communities by recognizing that when young people are fully engaged, they commit themselves to making positive change in their schools, in their communities, and in their own lives. Students are more motivated to take action when their voices are heard, their opinion matters, and their perspective is respected. SIAC works to identify and address pertinent student issues, thereby assuring that students remain central to all fiscal decisions. The council empowers students to develop character, compassion, civility, and community consciousness to ultimately make a better world.

Mission

Our mission is 1) to provide students with agency in order to contribute to a sense of self-worth, confidence, and the overall belief that they are agents for change and 2) to provide decision makers in our school district with focused and well-developed proposals aimed at impacting the areas of education that are of most importance to students.

Vision

The Student Inter-schools Action Council envisions a world where students take action to make a better world. To reach this vision, we work to:
  • Communicate with adults in the school district and community who make decisions that impact students
  • Take action through organizing projects and campaigns
  • Empower students to make positive change

About SIAC Feedback
